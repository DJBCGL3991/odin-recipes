Spicy Sausage Balls without Bisquick

Description

These are as good as Bisquick balls, without carbs and gluten. Eat fresh or refrigerate/freeze for later.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bulk spicy pork sausage
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 2 tbsp coconut flour
  • 2 0.25 oz. packages unflavored gelatin
  • 1 large egg
  • 0.5 teaspoon baking powder

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 F. Line jelly roll pan with parchment paper.
  2. Combine sausage, cheese, almond flour, coconut flour, gelatin, egg, and baking powder in large bowl. Mix. Form mixture in uniformly sized balls and place on pan.
  3. Bake in oven until cooked through, around 28 minutes. Remove balls and let cool on pan until oil reabsorbed (about 5 minutes).
