Hot Spinach Artichoke Dip

Description

Cheesy and fragrant. So delicous you won't even know it has artichokes!

Ingredients

  • 1 block cream cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Clove of minced garlic
  • Spices and seasonings
  • One can drained and chopped artichoke hearts
  • Thawed and drained frozen spinach

Directions

  1. Mix all indredients except mozzarella together
  2. Transfer mixture to baking dish and top with cheese
  3. Bake spinach artichoke dip in preheated oven until ready
